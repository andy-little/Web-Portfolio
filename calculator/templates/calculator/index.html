Welcome to Money Squared

A web app for managing your household outgoings

Money Squared is a web app for keeping track of your shared household expenses. Each member of the household can quickly and easily record any payment they have made for the benefit of the house eg a water bill or some cleaning products. Our nifty algorithm calculates who owes whom what so that you don’t have to.

Create Your Household

To get started, click register to create an account. Choose a name for your household and add 2-6 members.

Add A Transaction

Navigate to the transaction page and fill in the details of a purchase made by a member of your group.

View/Edit

Once an item has been added, use the transaction table to toggle through your complete transaction history. Unsettled transactions can be deleted or edited. The balances tab shows how much each member has either overpaid or underpaid.

Get Squared

Easily view who owes whom what. Once your debts have been settled, hit the big Get Squared button to bring everyone’s balance back to 0.
