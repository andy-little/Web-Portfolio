money squared

An app I built for managing household bills and other shared costs.

Users first create a household. Members of this household can add their transactions to the group. Members can also:
  • View their entire transaction history
  • Easily see who owes whom what
  • Settle debts
  • Edit/delete transactions not yet settled
In this project I created my own paginator api which, unlike Django's, can be used asynchronously.

personal


I’m Andy and I’m 21 years old expressed in hexadecimal. My strengths are that I am a fast learner and I’m adaptable. My weaknesses are that I’m a perfectionist and I work too hard. Wink!

When I was young I liked to take things apart and try to put them back together again. At 13 I taught myself HTML and a bit of C. I delighted in starting up my friends Windows 95 machines in the command line and renaming their password file. At 16 I went to study at the Academy of Contemporary Music where I earned a degree. I pursued a career in music. I taught, composed, produced, performed and ran a band. I learnt to use Adobe Creative Suite to create album art, posters, flyers and other promo material. But the music industry was tough, and most fulfilling work was poorly paid. It was for this reason that after 14 years I decided to return to my first love, computers.
