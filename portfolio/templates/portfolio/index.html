About Me

personal


I’m Andy and I’m 21 years old expressed in hexadecimal. My strengths are that I am a fast learner and I’m adaptable. My weaknesses are that I’m a perfectionist and I work too hard. Wink!

When I was young I liked to take things apart and try to put them back together again. At 13 I taught myself HTML and a bit of C. I delighted in starting up my friends Windows 95 machines in the command line and renaming their password file. At 16 I went to study at the Academy of Contemporary Music where I earned a degree. I pursued a career in music. I taught, composed, produced, performed and ran a band. I learnt to use Adobe Creative Suite to create album art, posters, flyers and other promo material. The music industry was a rewarding experience, but after 14 years I am looking for new challenges and a more stable position. It is for this reason I have decided to return to my first love, computers.
